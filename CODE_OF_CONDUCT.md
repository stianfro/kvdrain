# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

Be respectful, constructive, and considerate. Welcome people of every background and experience level. Discuss technical choices without personal attacks.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, deliberate intimidation, publishing private information, and repeated disruption of project work.

## Enforcement

Report conduct problems privately to the repository owner through the contact method on the owner's GitHub profile. Reports will be reviewed promptly and kept private as far as practical. The maintainer may edit or remove content, warn a participant, impose a temporary ban, or impose a permanent ban based on the impact and pattern of behavior.

This policy applies in repository spaces and when someone represents the project in public.
